## ğŸ” Key Findings

### **1. Cheque Deposit Holds**
- The largest issue in deposits/withdrawals  
- **23% of complaints** relate to cheque deposit holds  
- Customers frequently lose access to critical income (rent, bills, emergencies)

**Opportunities**
- Use a **risk-scoring model** to shorten hold times for low-risk, long-tenured accounts  
- Provide **real-time app or SMS updates** explaining hold status and expected release time

- The dataset underrepresents service-related complaints due to structural limitations
